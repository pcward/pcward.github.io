About

Hacker-scientist-type product manager.

Experienced in quantitative/qualitative analytics for product management, as well as A/B testing and experimental design for UX/UI. Strong affinity for communicating visually and in a clear, precise, and actionable manner. Fluent in Python, SQL, Stata, Tableau.

Winner of the 2014 Optimizely Optie Award in the category for “Best Advanced Feature Use”, finalist for “Most Interesting Integration,” together with my co-worker Rachel Johnson.

I have a BA in Economics from Virginia Tech, an MS in Information Systems from Cal State Sacramento, and I did 3.5 years of a PhD program in Information Systems (not completed) at the University of Utah.

I grew up in Asker, Norway, and am a big fan of Hokies football. I’m also an amateur bike racer.
